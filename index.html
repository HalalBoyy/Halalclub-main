About

I'm Abdulmalik Ibrahim, a Nigerian string artist and founder of HalalClub. I create detailed portraits and intricate visual patterns using thread, nails, and wood.

Over time, my work has given me the opportunity to present artworks to notable figures including Dr. Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, King Ateke Tom, Wizard Chan, Xeus, among others.

I'm currently working towards a Guinness World Record at Ahmadu Bello University with my team at HalalClub for the "largest collaborative string art by number of participants" with a focus on promoting creativity and inclusion among young women.
